About Keegan

Keegan Drake was a corporate lawyer at one of the most prestigious firms in the country, when an unexpected death in the family showed him to the complexity of estate planning and management. With his clients today, Keegan combines the skills he learned at a large law firm with the principles that guided him when helping his own family.

Keegan belongs to the Trusts & Estates section of the Colorado Bar Association. He earned a JD/MBA from Duke University, where he was an editor of the law review. He holds law licenses in Colorado and New York, and is a licensed real estate broker in Colorado.
